# MALTO Workshop Materials 2025

This repo contains the `notebooks` and `datasets` provided during the *Machine Learning workshops* hosted by the **MAchine Learning @ PoliTO (MALTO)** student team during the 2025 academic year.

---

## Workshop Topics Covered

### Workshop 1:
-   **Data Exploration:** Focused on loading, visualizing, and analyzing datasets to discover patterns.
-   **Classification:** Building and training models to categorize data.

### Workshop 2:
-   **Regression:** Developing models to predict continuous numerical values.
-   **Intro to Deep Learning:** Fundamental concepts of neural networks and their applications.
